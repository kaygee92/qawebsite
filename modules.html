There are three modules that are made of seven weeks each. Each module is designed to help the trainees learn more technical skills. In Mod One, trainees are taught the foundation of technology and how to maneuver throughout the workplace. Trainees take trainings in Technology Foundations, Web Development, Professional Skills and Business Communications. Module Two is building upon the skills of the individual skills that the trainees may have developed. The trainings that the trainees have are System Administrations, IT Help desk, along with the Pro Skills and Business Communications. Mod 3 is the mod that trainees are focused on the tracks (Cyber Security, Quality Assurance, Business Operations or Systems Administrations) that they are divided into.

Module 3 in QA the trainees learn about how to find bugs in the specifications and how to make a correct bug report. There are many other things that the trainees will be learning. Trainees learn Python, which will help them to learn Automation, which is a skill that many companies want from interns. They also learn the many different methodologies of Software Deelopment Life Cycle(SDLC).
